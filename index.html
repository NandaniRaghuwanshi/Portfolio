About Me
A little about who I am

I'm an AI Engineer based in Indore. I did my dual B.Tech + M.Tech in AI & Data Science from Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya and graduated with a CGPA of 8.40.

For the past year+ I've been at VSN International, where I get to do the full ride — sit in pre-sales meetings, understand what clients actually need, build AI POCs that prove the idea works, and then help ship it into production. It's messy and I love it.

My day-to-day usually involves Azure OpenAI, building RAG pipelines, wiring up AI agents with LangChain or CrewAI, writing Python that talks to things, and occasionally explaining to non-technical folks why the AI said that.

Outside of work I'm deepening my agent orchestration skills and exploring what LangGraph can do for complex multi-step workflows.
